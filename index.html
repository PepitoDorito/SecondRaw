The process

From their loading dock
to your workshop.

  1. 01

    Companies list surplus

    Factories, workshops and closing businesses tell us what they have — offcuts, overstock, machinery, whole inventories.

  2. 02

    We inspect & grade

    Our team checks quantity, quality and provenance, then grades each lot A–C so buyers know exactly what they’re getting.

  3. 03

    You buy & we deliver

    Buy by the lot, pallet or metre at a fraction of retail. We handle logistics, paperwork and delivery to your door.

Why it matters

Good for your budget.
Better for the planet.

Every tonne of material that finds a second life is a tonne that never becomes landfill — and a tonne that never has to be produced from scratch.

  • 12,400 t CO₂e avoided versus producing the same materials new.
  • €3.1M returned to companies for materials and equipment they’d have paid to dispose of.
  • 2,900+ projects built with rescued materials — from cafés to concert halls.
